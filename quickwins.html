Quick Wins

Try these right now — each takes 5 minutes or less

01

Summarize Any Article

Copy-paste any article into AI and ask for a summary. Try it with a news article, a Wikipedia page, or a textbook chapter.

"Summarize this article in 5 bullet points, highlighting the key takeaways for a student:"
~2 minutes
02

Create Study Flashcards

Give AI your study topic and get instant flashcards. Perfect for exam prep.

"Create 10 flashcards (question on front, answer on back) about [your topic]. Make them clear and concise."
~3 minutes
03

Translate & Learn

Paste text in any language and ask AI to translate it AND explain the grammar.

"Translate this to [language] and explain 3 interesting grammar points for a beginner:"
~2 minutes
04

Generate a Workout

Get a personalized workout routine based on what equipment you have (or none at all).

"Create a 20-minute workout I can do at home with no equipment. Include warm-up, exercises, and cool-down."
~1 minute
05

Write a Social Post

Need an Instagram caption or tweet? Tell AI the vibe and let it create options.

"Write 3 Instagram captions for a photo of [describe your photo]. Make them fun, engaging, and include relevant hashtags."
~1 minute
06

Debug Your Code

Paste your broken code and AI will find the bug and explain the fix.

"Find the bug in this code and explain what went wrong and how to fix it:"
~3 minutes
07

Create a Recipe

Tell AI what ingredients you have and get a recipe. Great for when you don't know what to cook.

"I have these ingredients: [list ingredients]. Suggest a simple, delicious recipe I can make in 30 minutes or less."
~2 minutes
08

Plan Your Weekend

Let AI help you plan a fun, balanced weekend with activities, study time, and rest.

"Help me plan my weekend. I need to study [subject], want to have fun, and also need to relax. Create a balanced schedule."
~2 minutes
