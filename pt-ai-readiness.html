💻 Technical Readiness

My organization has the digital infrastructure (reliable internet, modern devices, cloud access) needed to support AI tools.

I have experience using digital tools for productivity (e.g., project management apps, cloud collaboration, automation).

I feel comfortable experimenting with new software tools and can usually figure them out without extensive training.

🌱 Cultural Readiness

Leadership at my organization actively supports and encourages the adoption of new technologies, including AI.

My colleagues are generally open to changing their workflows and trying new approaches to their work.

Our organization has a culture of continuous learning, where people are encouraged to develop new skills regularly.

📜 Policy Readiness

My organization has clear policies regarding data privacy, security, and the acceptable use of third-party digital tools.

There are established guidelines or processes for evaluating and adopting new technology solutions in my workplace.

I understand (or my organization addresses) the ethical considerations of using AI, such as bias, transparency, and accountability.

🚀 Skills Readiness

I am confident in my digital literacy skills — I can effectively use digital platforms, evaluate online information, and communicate digitally.

I have already experimented with at least one AI tool (e.g., ChatGPT, Copilot, Midjourney, Gemini) for work or personal use.

I (or my organization) have the time and resources to invest in structured AI training and skills development.
