About the 100+ Things to Do with AI

Why this project exists — and the philosophy behind it

AI is not about doing the same faster and easier — it is about doing MORE and BETTER than before. And about learning to do new things and improve ourselves.

The conversation around Artificial Intelligence too often centers on replacement — which jobs will disappear, which tasks machines will take over. We believe that framing misses the point entirely.

The true power of AI is not about replacing humans. It is about augmenting our capabilities — expanding what we can think, create, build, and achieve. When we stop asking "what can AI do instead of me?" and start asking "what can I do with AI that I never could before?", everything changes.

That is why we built 100+ Things with AI. This site is a living collection of real, practical ways that AI lets you do more and better than before — write things you couldn’t write, learn skills you never had time to learn, create art and music and video you never imagined possible, and solve problems that once felt out of reach.

Every example here is proof of the same idea: the benefit is not doing the same thing faster. The benefit is doing things you simply could not do before. Learning new skills. Improving yourself. Reaching further.

Not sure where to start? Our Explore page offers 12 curated learning routes — from Student Explorer to Creative Artist, from Developer to Family Connection. Take a quick quiz for personalized recommendations, or browse routes tailored to your goals and experience level.

This is not a static or one-sided catalog. It is a living, community-driven collection that grows with its readers. You can like the items that resonate with you, suggest new do’s and don’ts, propose entirely new categories, or share example prompts and ideas. Every contribution helps shape this resource for everyone.

It is an invitation to grow — to discover what becomes possible when human creativity meets AI as a partner, not a replacement.
