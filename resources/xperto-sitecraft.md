# Xperto SiteCraft

## OVERVIEW

Xperto SiteCraft is a comprehensive AI-assisted web design framework that defines the soul of a website before touching its skin. Our methodology provides clarity, purpose, and creative direction that guides design, tone, structure, and every component of each site and project.

### Our Approach to Web Design

We don't just build websites. We craft digital experiences that connect brands with their audiences across languages and cultures. Our expert strategy combines deep human conceptualization with cutting-edge interactivity to create websites that are not only visually stunning but also highly effective.

Every project begins with understanding the essence of your brand. We define the soul of the website before crafting its skin. This gives us clarity, purpose, and the creative direction that guides designs, tones and structures every component of the site and project.

### Our AI Integration Philosophy

Beyond just using AI to do things faster and easier—we leverage AI to do things better, do more things, and do new things. That's our exponential advantage.

AI does not replace our teams or merely automates simple tasks or complex processes. 

Our human experts take care of the creative process while AI diligently executes with blazing speed, astounding detail and technical precision our original vision. Together our human experts and AI are able to explore possibilities and stretch the boundaries of creativity and productivity.

## WORKFLOW ARCHITECTURE

### Phase Gates with Deliverables

The Xperto SiteCraft model follows a gated workflow where each phase must produce specific deliverables and meet validation criteria before proceeding.

#### Phase 1: Discovery

##### Duration

1-2 weeks (varies by project complexity)

##### Activities

* Stakeholder interviews and vision gathering  
* Market and competitor research  
* Business objective definition  
* Initial scope and outcome setting

##### Exit Deliverables

| Deliverable | Owner | Validation Method |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Discovery Brief | Project Manager | Client sign-off |
| Stakeholder Interview Summaries | Business Strategist | Internal review |
| Competitive Analysis | Business Strategist | Team review |
| Initial Scope Document | Project Manager | Client alignment |

##### Gate Criteria

- [ ] All key stakeholders interviewed  
- [ ] Business objectives clearly articulated  
- [ ] Target audience initially identified  
- [ ] Competitive landscape understood  
- [ ] Client sign-off on Discovery Brief

#### Phase 2: Conceptualization

##### Duration

2-3 weeks

##### Activities

* Brand DNA development  
* Voice Guidelines creation  
* Persona development  
* Value proposition articulation  
* Communication strategy definition

##### Exit Deliverables

| Deliverable | Owner | Validation Method |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Brand DNA Document | Brand Strategist | Client alignment review |
| Voice Guidelines | Communications Expert | Brand consistency check |
| Target Audience Personas | Market Research Specialist | Client validation |
| Website Goals & Value Propositions | Website Strategy Consultant | Strategic alignment score |
| Strategic Foundation Package | Project Manager | Comprehensive review |

##### Gate Criteria

- [ ] Brand DNA Document approved  
- [ ] Voice Guidelines aligned with Brand DNA  
- [ ] Personas validated by client  
- [ ] Value propositions clearly differentiated  
- [ ] All experts aligned on strategic direction

#### Phase 3: Design

##### Duration

3-5 weeks

##### Activities

* Content architecture development  
* Creative direction establishment  
* Visual design system creation  
* Flavor system development  
* UX/UI design  
* SEO strategy integration

##### Exit Deliverables

| Deliverable | Owner | Validation Method |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Information Architecture | Content Strategist | User flow validation |

| Visual Design System | Visual Design Expert | Consistency audit |
| Flavor System Specifications | Visual Design Expert | Flexibility testing |

- [ ] Information architecture supports all user journeys

### Iteration Protocols

#### Micro-Iterations (Within Phase)

Each expert produces drafts that receive feedback from adjacent experts before finalization:

```
Expert Draft → Adjacent Expert Review → Refinement → Validation → Finalization
```

##### Examples

* Brand Strategist draft → Communications Expert review → Brand DNA refinement  
* Creative Designer concepts → Visual Design Expert feasibility check → Creative direction finalization  
* UX Wireframes → UI Expert implementation review → Wireframe refinement

#### Macro-Iterations (Between Phases)

Insights from later phases can trigger reviews in earlier phases:

```
Execution Insight → Design Review → Conceptualization Adjustment (if needed)
```

##### Triggers for Macro-Iteration

* Technical constraints discovered during execution  
* User testing reveals conceptual misalignment  
* Performance requirements impact design decisions  
* Client feedback requires strategic adjustment

#### Retrospective Protocol

At each phase gate, conduct a brief retrospective:

* What worked well?  
* What could be improved?  
* What learnings apply to future phases?  
* What adjustments needed for this project?

### Code Style Guidelines

#### HTML Standards

* 4-space indentation (consistent throughout)  
* Semantic HTML5 elements  
* Meaningful `class` names (BEM or similar methodology)  
* `data-` attributes for JavaScript hooks  
* `data-i18n` attributes for translatable content  
* Accessibility attributes (`aria-`, `role`, `alt`)  
* Comments for major sections

#### CSS Standards

* Mobile-first responsive approach  
* CSS custom properties for theming  
* BEM naming convention for components  
* Logical property grouping:  
  1. Layout (display, position, flex/grid)  
  2. Box model (margin, padding, border)  
  3. Typography (font, text, color)  
  4. Visual (background, shadow, opacity)  
  5. Animation (transition, transform)

#### JavaScript Standards

* ES6+ syntax with appropriate transpilation  
* Modular structure with clear namespacing  
* Event delegation where possible  
* Meaningful variable and function names  
* JSDoc comments for public methods  
* Error handling and graceful degradation

### Page Structure Template

#### PAGE ELEMENTS

For each page, the workflow defines:

* **Title** — Page title and SEO title  
* **Byline or Subtitle** — Supporting headline  
* **Hero Image/Video** — Primary visual  
* **Goal of the page** — What this page must accomplish  
* **Nature of the page** — Informative, interactive, sales, support, institutional, reference, etc.  
* **Introductory paragraph** — Opening content  
* **Sections of the page** — Content blocks with hierarchy  
* **Media elements** — Images, videos, graphics needed  
* **External reference links** — Outbound links  
* **Internal links and CTAs** — Navigation and conversion elements  
* **SEO specifications** — Keywords, metadata, schema  
* **Accessibility requirements** — ARIA labels, alt text, etc.

### The Flavor System

We have developed the concept of "Flavors" as a way to customize the presentation of the brand while respecting its corporate and visual guidelines to optimize your communication impact. This allows us to create really creative and unique pages.

Flavors are thematic visual variations that can be applied to your website to adapt to seasons, special occasions, audience segments or specific moods while maintaining brand consistency and without affecting the content.

#### Flavor Architecture Framework

##### Flavor Layers

1. **Chromatic Layer** — Color palette variations  
2. **Typographic Layer** — Font weight/style adjustments (within brand fonts)  
3. **Imagery Layer** — Photography/illustration style shifts  
4. **Motion Layer** — Animation character changes  
5. **Tonal Layer** — Copy micro-adjustments

##### Flavor Types

They can be as diverse and specific as Independence Day, Breast Cancer Awareness Month, Urban Explorer, Digital Scribe, Future Forward, Artisanal Roots, Tranquil Haven

| Type | Trigger | Duration | Scope | Example |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Seasonal** | Calendar | Weeks-months | Site-wide | Summer, Winter, Spring |
| **Event-based** | Specific date | Days-weeks | Site-wide or section | Black Friday, Product Launch |
| **Cultural** | Regional/cultural moments | Variable | Localized or site-wide | Lunar New Year, Carnival |
| **Contextual** | User segment | Persistent | Personalized | Executive version, Youth version |
| **Promotional** | Campaign | Campaign duration | Landing pages | Sale, Special Offer |
| **Thematic** | Brand expression | Ongoing | Alternative site version | Playful vs. Professional |

##### Flavor Inheritance Rules

* **Base Flavor:** Default brand expression (always defined)  
* **Overlay Flavors:** Additive modifications to Base (stackable)  
* **Override Flavors:** Complete replacement for specific contexts
