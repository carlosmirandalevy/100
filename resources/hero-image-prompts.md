# Hero Image Prompts for 100.cemi.ai

## MidJourney Prompt

```
A stunning wide-angle conceptual illustration of a luminous human figure standing at the center of an expansive circular mosaic of floating holographic panels, each panel depicting a different creative pursuit — writing, painting, making music, coding, scientific discovery, family activities, video editing, business charts — all radiating outward like a starburst. The scene is set against a deep midnight navy background (#0a0a1a) with warm glowing orbs of crimson red, antique gold, and slate blue drifting through the space. The number "100" subtly embedded in the composition as a translucent watermark of light. The human figure reaches upward with both hands, streams of golden and red light flowing from their fingertips into the panels. Atmosphere of wonder, possibility, and empowerment. Rich color palette of deep red (#a70000), warm gold (#D9A441), muted teal-blue (#2F4F6F), and sage green (#4B775A). Cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed, editorial illustration style blending 3D render with painterly textures --ar 16:9 --s 750 --v 6.1
```

## Gemini (Imagen 3 / Veo) Prompt

```
Create a wide cinematic editorial illustration for a website hero image. The concept: a diverse group of people of different ages and backgrounds standing together in a semicircle, each person engaged in a different AI-powered activity — one is writing on a glowing tablet, another is composing music with floating holographic notes, one is painting on a digital canvas with AI brushstrokes materializing beside them, another is reviewing scientific data visualizations, one is playing with their child and an AI assistant, another is coding with luminous lines of code streaming upward. They all stand on a reflective dark surface against a deep navy-to-dark-purple gradient background. Above and around them, abstract flowing ribbons of light in crimson red, antique gold, slate blue, and sage green intertwine and connect all the people, symbolizing the "100+ things" they can do with AI. Scattered throughout the scene are softly glowing translucent orbs in those same warm colors. The overall mood is aspirational, warm, inclusive, and forward-looking — not cold or robotic. The style should be polished editorial illustration with a slight 3D quality, rich textures, and cinematic depth of field. Widescreen 16:9 aspect ratio. No text in the image.
```

## Comparison

| | MidJourney | Gemini |
|---|---|---|
| **Focus** | Single symbolic figure + abstract mosaic panels | Diverse group of people doing concrete activities |
| **Style** | More abstract/conceptual, relies on MJ's aesthetic strength | More literal/narrative, leverages Imagen's realism |
| **Composition** | Radial starburst from center | Semicircle group scene |
| **Tone** | Ethereal, aspirational | Inclusive, human-centered |

Both use the site's color palette (`#a70000`, `#D9A441`, `#2F4F6F`, `#4B775A`), dark background aesthetic, floating orbs from the hero section, and the breadth of categories the site covers.
